Safety Information

Dosage & Precautions

Always take medicines as advised by your doctor or pharmacist.

Dosage

  • Adults: 1 tablet every 6-8 hours if needed.
  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • Take with water after food if advised.
  • Follow your doctor's instructions.

Possible Side Effects

  • Nausea
  • Skin rash (rare)
  • Allergic reaction (rare)
  • Seek medical help if symptoms are severe.

Storage

  • Store below 30°C.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Do not use after expiry date.

Safety Warning

Do not consume alcohol while taking this medicine. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult a doctor before use. Avoid self-medication for long periods.
